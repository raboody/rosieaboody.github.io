Rosie Aboody

Hi! I'm a fourth year graduate student in the Yale Psychology Department. I'm currently a member of Dr. Julian Jara-Ettinger's Computation and Cognitive Development Lab, and Dr. Frank Keil's Cognitive Development Lab. I'm interested in social cognition broadly, but in the bulk of my work I study how children infer what someone knows or believes, simply from observing that person act in the world. See below for descriptions of three current lines of work.

How do we infer others' mental states from their actions?

How do we deal with social information?

How do we transmit information to others?
